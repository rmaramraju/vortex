Ask A Question

How much dev effort is needed to integrate VORTEX?

Very little. Our SDK can be embedded into your website or app in less than a week, and our team handles onboarding end-to-end.

Does VORTEX store any customer data?

No. The customer owns their clone. VORTEX simply provides secure access and encryption — your brand accesses only what’s needed for try-on.

Will users need to rescan for our brand?

Not if they already have a VORTEX profile. Their clone syncs instantly, improving time-to-try and trial rates.

Can we customize the interface to match our brand?

Yes. UI is fully customizable, and we provide both branded and white-label options.

What kind of analytics do we get access to?

You get product-level try-on rates, conversion attribution, drop-off points, outfit save trends, and geo-level demand heatmaps.
